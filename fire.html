How the FIRE Calculation Works

The Destination: The 4% Rule

Your "Target Number" is based on your Monthly Expenses. The 4% Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR) suggests that if you have invested 25 times your annual expenses, you can live off the returns for life without depleting your principal.

The Velocity: Savings Rate

The speed at which you retire is dictated by your Savings Rate. The gap between what you earn and what you spend is the fuel that drives your portfolio growth via compound interest.
