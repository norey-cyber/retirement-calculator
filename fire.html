How the FIRE Calculation Works

The Destination: The 4% Rule

Your "Target Number" is calculated using the 4% Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR). This suggests that if you have 25x your annual expenses invested, you can sustain your lifestyle for decades.

The Velocity: Savings Rate

How fast you retire depends on your Savings Rate—the gap between your earnings and spending. This extra cash is invested and accelerated by compound interest.
