The Math of Early Retirement

This calculator uses two primary figures to determine your freedom date:

  • The Destination (FIRE Number): This is calculated by taking your Monthly Expenses and multiplying by 12, then dividing by your Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR). This is based on the famous 4% rule.
  • The Velocity (Savings): Your Monthly Savings determines how fast you fill up that bucket. The more you save relative to what you spend, the earlier you reach freedom.
