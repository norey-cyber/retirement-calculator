Understanding the FIRE Movement

What is a FIRE Number?

Your FIRE number is the total amount of invested assets you need to cover your annual expenses indefinitely. Most followers of the movement use the Rule of 25: Multiplying your annual expenses by 25.

The 4% Rule

The Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR) is the percentage of your portfolio you can withdraw each year without running out of money. 4% is the historical gold standard based on the Trinity Study.
